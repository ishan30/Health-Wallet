Today, suppose what happens when we are on a trip and unluckily met with an accident. At that time what if in case we need our previous medical reports, previous medical prescriptions, lab reports etc. At that time obviously as we are on trip we doesn’t have our previous medical reports, prescriptions, lab reports etc. with us.

This is the problem we are facing, I had taken an example of trip, but anything can happen we don’t know and at that time it will be easy for doctor to take any judgement if our all past records are there with us stored online . So our project mainly focus to store medical details of patient and that will be easy for both doctors and patients.


Health Wallet is an app/website, which is used to record every patient’s medical history in terms of reports, prescription, results etc. Unique ID and Password is created for every patient. A Health Card is also provided to every patient which contains some basic details of patient like Blood Group, his/her Allergies, heart problems etc.
